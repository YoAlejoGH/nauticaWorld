REASON FOR CHOICE

FORTRESS

The 'NAVIERO REF586' life jacket is the only life jacket in Colombia approved by the technical standard NTMD-0018-A3. Its fabrics are 100% waterproof, and its materials that enable the jacket to float are entirely recyclable.

WEAKNESS

A weakness that this product may have in exports is accommodating prices in other countries, shipping, taxes, delivery time, unfamiliarity with the laws of other countries, and the absence of professional knowledge in internationalization.

OPPORTUNITY

Besides having significant opportunities for internationalization, they also have many opportunities for expanding their market. There are numerous niches to explore, but concerning internationalization, there are abundant opportunities. On several occasions, countries like Mexico and Ecuador were contacted for deals, business, and commercial exchanges.

THREAT

Legislation changes, another pandemic, a competitor with greater financial capital, risk of in-house manufacturing.

RELATIONSHIP WITH ENVIRONMENTAL

ECONOMICA

It's a company that has a strong commitment to the national economy as it contributes to the GDP, boosts the economy, with a profit margin between 70% and 80%.

AMBIENTAL

Relationship with the environmental dimension: 100% waterproof fabrics, manufactured by La Fayette company, and the flotation material is 100% recyclable.

SOCIAL

The company employs individuals directly, creates indirect job opportunities, its workshops are run by single mothers, and it provides social security as all its products are designed to safeguard people's lives.
